RESOURCES
  • Postpartum Progress is a national nonprofit providing peer-to-peer support for women with perinatal mood and anxiety disorders like PPD. Programs include blog with 2.6 million visitors per year, a private forum, an annual conference, social media connection, local events and more.
  • Postpartum Support International (PSI), is an international organization focused on support, education, advocacy and research for perinatal mental health
  • Marce Society, an international organization to promote, facilitate and communicate about research and all aspects of the mental health of women, their children, and partners around the time of childbirth
  • Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA), US government agency focused on child health
  • Suicide Prevention Lifeline, an emergency helpline for those experiencing thoughts of self-harm or extreme depression
