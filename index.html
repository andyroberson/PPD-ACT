Postpartum Depression: Action Towards Causes and Treatment
PPD ACT aims to understand why some women get postpartum depression (PPD) and others do not.

We are an international group of dedicated clinicians and scientists who are committed to understanding the interaction of genes and environment to predict which women are at risk of PPD. This will allow for the development of preventive strategies and targeted treatment that would dramatically decrease suffering for women and their families.

Join our study to help find the cure.

The Study

We are conducting a study to understand more about the genetic basis of postpartum depression and postpartum psychosis. Our goal is to learn about the reasons why some women get postpartum depression or psychosis and other women do not.

Using the Apple Research Kit we will recruit 100,000 women from around the world to participate in our app.

Learn more about the study
Data collected through the app will be studied and will help us figure out the causes and develop new treatments.
Hopefully, we can one day prevent postpartum disorders from happening in the first place.
